ALL OF US ARE DEAD

All of Us Are Dead (Korean: 지금 우리 학교는).It is zombie apocalypse horror streaming television series.The series centers on a group of high school students in South Korean city of Hyosan, and their struggle to survive amidst a zombie outbreak. It is based on the Naver webtoon of the same name by Joo Dong-geun,which was published between 2009 and 2011.

TWINKLING WATERMELON

Twinkling Watermelon (Korean: 반짝이는 워터멜론).It tells the story of a CODA boy, who travels back in time to 1995. He joins a band with his childhood father and forms a band called Watermelon Sugar with the suspicious youths he meets there, and communicates with them through music.

HAPPINESS

Happiness (Korean: 해피니스; RR: Haepiniseu).It is an apocalyptic thriller that takes place in a time in which infectious diseases have become the new norm.The release of a failed treatment drug "Next" has caused a worldwide pandemic known as the Lytta Virus, a.k.a. "mad person disease".It premiered on tvN on November 5, 2021 and aired every Friday and Saturday at 22:40 (KST) for 12 episodes.

ALCHEMY OF SOULS

Alchemy of Souls (Korean: 환혼).It is a South Korean television series written by the Hong sisters,It depicts the stories of young mages dealing with heaven and earth.Set in a fictional country called Daeho,The series is about the love and growth of young mages as they overcome their twisted fates due to a forbidden magic spell known as the "alchemy of souls", which allows souls to switch bodies.

TWENTY FIVE TWENTY ONE

Twenty-Five Twenty-One (Korean: 스물다섯 스물하나).The series depicts the romantic lives of five characters spanning from 1998 to 2021.It premiered on tvN on February 12, 2022 for 16 episodes.

MOVING

Moving (Korean: 무빙).Based on the eponymous webtoon by Kang Full, The series is a supernatural drama that deals with three teenager high school students and their parents who discover their super powers.Moving after facing delays in production, premiered on Disney+ on August 9, 2023.

BUSINESS PROPOSAL

Business Proposal (Korean: 사내맞선).is a South Korean romantic comedy television series based on the webtoon of the same title.It tells the story of Shin Ha-ri, an employee who accepts to go on a blind date in place of her friend, but finds out that her date is actually her boss. The series aired for 12 episodes on SBS TV.

SQUID GAME

Squid Game (Korean: 오징어 게임).The series revolves around a secret contest where 456 players, all of whom are in deep financial hardship, risk their lives to play a series of deadly children's games for the chance to win a ₩45.6 billion prize. The series' title draws from a Korean children's game.It became Netflix's most-watched series.
